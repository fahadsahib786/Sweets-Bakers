    About us

    EXPLORE MORE ABOUT US

    At Bismillah Sweets & Bakers, our mission is to consistently provide quality food products, using fresh ingredients from local and environmentally sustainable suppliers when possible, and to be a good neighbor to the people of Khan pur and the rest of Pakistan.Bismillah Sweets & Bakers was created in 1973 as the Desi Mithai shop , but later after a decade the name came under a Muslim Family from Khan pur, who entered this business in 1990s and in year 2000 the addition of bakery was done to the name of Bismillah Sweets & Bakers, and now is one of the pioneers in the field of Sweets and Bakers, by providing the customers the same taste and service from the history of more than 45 years.
